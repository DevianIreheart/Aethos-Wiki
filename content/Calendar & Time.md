**Etharia** is not a planet, but a moon in orbit around the massive world of [[Sanguis]], commonly referred to as the "blood moon" or "big red." This red planet dominates the skies of Etharia for the half of the year it is visible. In summer, it glows crimson throughout the night, bathing the land in blood‑red twilight. In winter, it looms as a vast dark sphere that blots out whole swathes of stars. Etharia has its own smaller companion moon, [[Luna]], whose 21‑day cycle provides the rhythm for weeks and many holy days.

# Aethorian Calendars & Timekeeping

## Mercian Calendar 
A year is defined by Etharia’s 360 day orbit around Sanguis. The Mercian calendar  divides the year into twelve months of thirty days each, beginning and ending with the solar eclipse wherein Sanguis completely blots out the sun for 20 days. When Sanguis lies between Etharia and the sun the days grow colder, and when Etharia passes between Sanguis and the sun they grow warmer. Thus winter and summer are not caused by axial tilt but by shifts in orbital position relative to the sun.

**Zodiac Months (12 × 30 days)**: Chimeris, Hydris, Phoenix, Pegasis, Tytonis, Griffonis, Draconis, Carcharodus, Pantherus, Ursus, Corvus, Cerberus

**Moons**: [[Luna]] (21-day cycle, true moon) and [[Sanguis]] (360-day cycle, actually a planet which Etharia orbits)

**Weekdays**: Moonday, Titheday, Oathday, Queensday, Favourday, Starday, Sunday. (Weeks follow the Lunar cycle - every 3rd Moonday coincides with the new moon.)

**Eras**: Conjuring → Age of Chaos → Enlightenment → Severance → Modern Age

### Seasons
- **Winter**: Cerberus 16 – Hydris 15
- **Spring**: Hydris 16 – Pegasis 15
- **Summer**: Pegasis 16 – Griffonis 30
- **Autumn**: Carcharodus 1 – Ursus 15
