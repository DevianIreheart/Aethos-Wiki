The **Black Sun** is a network of thieves, cutpurses, homeless, politicians, agents and innumerable informants. They are a realm-wide crime organization with an honourable mission but dubious methods. They believe themselves a check on the integrity and power of aristocrats and nobles, merchants and all those who would exploit the common folk.

### Foundation, Members & Structure
The network was founded by the ex-head of the [[Imperial Surveillance Division]] of [[Mercy Province]] in 1010 M.A., [[Lord Achus Remallion]]. His discontent with the imperial system was well known, and after years of struggle against laws he believed were designed only to serve the rich and powerful, he resigned and formed the **Black Sun**. 

**Agents are The Black Sun's permanent members**. They hail from many walks of life, but they share specialized skillsets in thievery, espionage, and undercover operations. They spend their time spying on nobles, trailing merchants, falsifying ledgers, and otherwise carrying out whatever clandestine tasks the Black Sun needs doing. Agents are also the contact points for informants. 

**Informants are loosely associated but not members** – they’re members of the public who feed information to agents in return for money. Reliable informants are hard to come by, and the network does not take well to any who muck them about. Should an informant prove trustworthy however, the good favour of the Black Sun is worth far more than its gold. Their exhaustive list of contacts has made them **the realm's premier middlemen for rare and exotic goods**, such as magic items and enchanted weapons. For the right price, the Black Sun can get nearly anything you desire. For a small fee, they’ll deliver to you as well – they know where you live, after all.

### Goals 
- **Expose and punish the unlawful** deeds of the powerful to the public, that nobility does not act without accountability
- Opulence is grotesque; **redistribute wealth** from those with too much to those with too little. Rob the rich, give to the poor 
- **Protect the common folk** and their freedoms, shelter the innocent from exploitation, bring justice to the guilty

### Runners
The Black Sun's vast network of informants, agents and contacts are organized into isolated, self-sustaining cells that have almost no communication with each other. Each cell has one or two members called **runners**, that are responsible for conveying information between cells. Via the runners, information eventually finds its way to the leader of the organization, who is called [[The Eclipse]]. Only The Eclipse may appoint a new runner - this personal touch helps ensure their loyalty is true.

### The Eclipse
Via their cells and runners, The Eclipse is privy to information about all the realm and, to an extent, its neighbours. If there is something of import happening anywhere in Mercy, you can be sure files are being kept on it somewhere, by someone, in the Black Sun's vast network, and should it prove important enough, The Eclipse will act on it. 


## Orwarld Slink
_Field agent. Halfling, Masc., Late 40’s_

A wily old fox serving the [[Black Sun]] as a contact, runner, and problem solver (fixer) in [[Kingdom of Mercy|Mercy]]'s capital, [[Revere]]. When not checking drop points or pigeon coops for information from his informants, [[Orwarld Slink|Orwarld]] spends his time in dive bars and disreputable locations, tailing nobles, working undercover on the staff of merchants, or even posing as a [[Reverent Guard|city guard]]. Always watching, listening, and exposing the misdeeds of the privileged, Orwarld has garnered a reputation as 'The Shadow that Talks'.

## Countess Marie Laveau
_Intelligencer. Human, Fem., Mid 20’s._

The exclusivity of the peerage means most spies don't fit in at tea parties, private gatherings with royals or day tripping to country estates in summer. Yet these private occasions are most important for the Black Sun to learn of, and for this there are insiders: Nobles willing to spy and tell on their peers, risking social and political exile were they to be discovered. Marie Laveau is of the rare stripe that take on this task, navigating the tedium of politics and aristocratic socialising to bring updates on Revere's most powerful to the attention of The Eclipse.

## Adventure Hooks

**Guild Conspiracy.** The heroes are tailed then contacted by an agent of the Black Sun. They bear a mission to infiltrate an adventuring guild and investigate a string of disappearances happening within…

**A Dubious Fortune.** A masquerade ball is staged by a newly appointed noble, recently come into a fortune some say was stolen, not earned. The heroes must attend the party and find evidence of any wrongdoing at the lord's manor…
