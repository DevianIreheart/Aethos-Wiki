**Aethos** is a major continent on [[Etharia]]. It and the surrounding waters comprise over a dozen distinct regions, ruled by over twenty-five sovereign realms. 

## Geography
Bounded by the [[Serpent Sea]] and [[Aescandia]] to the north, the [[Obsidian Sea]] and the [[Vast Ocean]] to the east, the [[Veil Ocean]] to the west, and the [[Antarctic Ocean]] to the south.

## Society
Aethos is divided into about twenty-five [[realm|realms]], of which the [[Empire of Phairos]] is the largest and most populous, spanning about 40% of the continent and comprising 15% of its population.
