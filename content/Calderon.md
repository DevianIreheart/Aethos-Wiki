**Calderon**, god of dragons, is the patron of knights, nobles, warriors, leaders, and the dutiful. Calderians believe that strength should be wielded to the betterment of the many, and are expected to strictly adhere to his [[Calderon#Tenets of Calderon|7 chivalric virtues]].

## Description
The gold dragon of truth, justice, honour and righteous warfare, Calderon is the primary deity in the [[Kingdom of Mercy]] and is popular across northern [[Aethos]].

**Followers.** Calderons adherents are often valorous knights, good-willed nobles, and selfless heroes, and they are especially popular amongst the folk of [[Tourelle]], where they are the religious majority. Calderons acolytes (Calderians) value altruism, integrity, and valour, and follow a [[Calderon#Tenets of Calderon|chivalric code of virtues]] which they all strive to champion.

**Temples.** Calderon’s temples are imposing and regal, with high spires and gleaming gold details, adorned with blue tapestries depicting the golden dragon profile.

**Depiction.** Calderon is typically shown as an untarnished gold dragon, towering over mortal dragons in size and splendour.

**Symbols.**  A golden shield, in the shape of dragon scale, represents Calderon’s divine protection and defence of justice. A sword, gleaming with the light of the sun, represents values of righteous warfare, valour, and martial prowess. Blue tapestries, lined or detailed with gold, are a symbol of Calderon’s divine presence.

## Worship
Followers pray daily at dawn to Calderon, asking for guidance, protection, and the strength to uphold his virtues. They strive to perform acts of valour in Calderon’s name, such as protecting the weak, standing up for justice, or showing mercy to defeated enemies. They routinely participate in tournaments, be they of martial contest, debates, or charitable competition. 

At least once in their lifetime, followers undergo a pilgrimage to Calderon’s mountain temple in central Mercy as a test of their devotion. Drakongard is the holy month of Calderon, and throughout its final week followers fast, eating only sparsely of bread and cheese to demonstrate their discipline and commitment. Acts of community service are a common way followers show devotion to Calderon’s tenets of generosity and mercy by helping the poor, healing the sick, or working for justice in their community. Followers spend much time in study of the Chivalric Code and other holy texts, striving to better understand and live out Calderon’s sacred teachings.

**Holy Day.** Oath Day falls on the last day of summer, the 5th of Drakengard. It is usually marked by a tourney, to which knights and adventurers flock to show off their martial skills to excited crowds of commoners and nobles. The capstone event is the swearing of oaths by all the faithful in attendance.

## Divine Opposition
[[Malis]], The Serpent of Shadows, is Calderon’s sworn foe and opposition within the [[Aethorian Orthodoxy|pantheon]]. As the deity of deceit, ambition, and tyranny, [[Malisites]] (followers of Malis) adhere to a corrupted set of tenets that are a darkened mirror to Calderon's, emphasising personal empowerment and believing order stems from the rule of a strong few over the weak masses.

>*Yet know this: Calderon hath an eternal adversary - **Malis, the Serpent of Shadows**, whose gospel is deceit, ambition, and tyranny. Thus the faithful say the world itself is framed by the strife between Calderon’s golden flame and Malis’ endless night*
>- Elliania de la Vale

## Tenets of Calderon
Follow this Chivalric Code whose virtues bring thou honour and are stated thus:

- **Courage:** Demonstrate bravery in the face of danger and difficulty, shirk not responsibility.
- **Justice:** Conduct thyself with decency and fairness, ensure justice cometh unto the wicked.
- **Mercy:** Grant mercy upon thy surrendered foes, and forgive the sins of the ignorant and the fool.
- **Generosity:** Be not selfish with thy possessions nor efforts. Giveth thy abundances unto the needy, lend thy strength unto the weak, thy shield unto the defenceless, and thy arms unto the feeble.
- **Faith:** Remain faithful to all oaths, promises and loyalties. Trust in others, have integrity and honesty always. 
- **Hope:** Carry thyself that hope be inspired in all who thy doth meet and in all who look upon thee in even brief passing
- **Nobility:** Uphold thy honour through truth, integrity, temperance, and humility. Stray not from thy virtues nor path of righteousness, under witness eternal by most glorious Calderon.
